बाल विकास एवं शिक्षण विधियाँ

Child Development & Pedagogy

★★★★★ PYQ Repeat Rate

★ PYQ Hot Topics

✦ Piaget's 4 Stages of Cognitive Development

✦ Vygotsky's ZPD + Scaffolding + MKO

✦ Thorndike's 3 Laws (Readiness, Exercise, Effect)

✦ Pavlov Classical vs Skinner Operant Conditioning

✦ Inclusive Education — Braille, identification of excluded groups

Full Syllabus

• Child development stages: physical, mental, emotional, language, creative

• Heredity & environment factors

• Thorndike, Pavlov, Skinner, Kohler, Piaget, Vygotsky theories

• Learning curve and plateau — meaning, causes, remedies

• Teaching methods, micro-teaching, basic skills, maxims

• Inclusive education: identification, TLMs, Braille, special methods

• Guidance and counselling: meaning, types, need, scope, institutions

Language I — Hindi

• Samas — all 6 types with Samas Vigrah

• Alankar: Anupras, Yamak, Shlesh, Upma, Rupak, Utpreksha, Atishayokti

• Vachan, Ling, Kaal; Upsarg, Pratyay; Tatsam/Tadbhav/Deshaj

• Muhavare & Lokoktiyan; Vachya; Sangya, Sarvanam, Visheshan, Kriya

45-दिन का प्लान

/ Tour Schedule
PH.
1
Week 1–2: NCERT Foundation / NCERT नींव

Read all relevant NCERT Class 1–8. No shortcuts — 70% questions base. One subject per day, 4–5 hrs. NCERT पहले पढ़ो, बाकी बाद में।

PH.
2
Week 3–4: Subject Book Deep Dive / विषय गहराई

Study from recommended books — CDP theories, Hindi grammar, English grammar, Maths formulae. 3–4 hrs theory + 1 hr practice daily.

PH.
3
Week 5–6: PYQ Battle Mode / PYQ अभ्यास

Solve PYQs year-by-year (2021 first). Note repeating topics. Build your personal "Hot List". 2 full papers per week + topic drills.

PH.
4
Week 7: Full Mock Test Mode / पूरा मॉक

1 full mock daily under real conditions (150 min + OMR simulation). Analyse errors, target weak areas. Confidence builds here.

PH.
5
Final Week: Rapid Revision Only / केवल दोहराना

Zero new topics. Quick notes: formulae, theorists, dates, grammar rules. 1 mock + sleep well. You are ready. नया कुछ मत सीखो।
